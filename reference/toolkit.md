# TOOLKIT - PM utilities

Standalone utilities that do not fit the discovery-to-launch arc: career and document help. Legal
drafts here are starting points, not legal advice - always flag review by counsel.

## review-resume

Critique a PM resume for impact and fit.
- **Bullets - XYZ+S:** "Accomplished X, measured by Y, by doing Z" + Scope. Every bullet leads
  with a result and a number, not a responsibility.
- **Checks:** quantified outcomes, PM keywords matched to target roles (discovery, experimentation,
  stakeholder management, metrics), seniority signal, clarity, length, formatting consistency.
- **Output:** prioritized fixes (highest-impact first) + 2-3 rewritten example bullets.

## tailor-resume

Align a resume to a specific job description: extract the JD's must-have skills/keywords, map the
candidate's real experience to them, and surface honest gaps. Output: targeted edits + a
keyword-coverage note. Never fabricate experience - reframe real work, do not invent it.

## draft-nda

Mutual or one-way NDA starting draft: parties, definition of confidential information, exclusions,
obligations, term + survival, return/destruction, governing law, remedies. Output: a clean draft
with bracketed placeholders + a clear "review with counsel before signing" note.

## privacy-policy

GDPR/CCPA-aware privacy policy draft: data collected, purposes + legal basis, sharing/processors,
retention, user rights (access/delete/portability/opt-out), cookies, international transfers,
contact/DPO, update process. Output: a structured draft with placeholders + a flag that
jurisdiction-specific review is required.

## grammar-check / proofread

Improve grammar, logic, and flow without changing meaning. Passes: correctness (grammar, spelling,
punctuation) -> clarity (wordiness, passive voice, ambiguity) -> logic/flow (transitions,
ordering) -> consistency (terms, tense, tone). Output: the corrected text + a short list of the
substantive changes (not every comma).

## Critic completeness anchors

Resume bullets are outcome-first and quantified; tailored resumes never fabricate experience;
legal drafts carry placeholders and an explicit counsel-review flag; proofreading preserves the
author's meaning and voice.
